About Me

My little story thus far...

I am thouroughly conviced, that I grew up in the most beautiful place in the whole world. At least to me it was the mot beaustiful, and until 2020 came to around to kinda flip my little SoCal world upside down that was all I knew.

Pinapple Curry, Palm Trees, and plenty of sunshine characterized my flip flop yeararound childhood and high school days. Some of my fondest high school memories are sitting under a certain tree next to over 100 year old Clock Auditorium everyday at lunch. In those days it seemed like nothing would ever change.

...But 2020, as for most, brought lots of changes. Goodbye high school, goodbye graduation, and Redlands, hello Covid and College!

I headed to Brigham Young Univerity for my freshman year of college and about a year later Seattle for my 18th month mission, for the CHurch of Jesus Christ of Later Day Saints.

Since the year 2020 my sights have greatly expanded. I've gained experience, perspective, and have had my beliefs and veiw of the world challenged.

Redlands California will always be my first home, but my other places of residence have shaped and meant just as much to me in recent years.
